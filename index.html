    • The Atlantic Salmon

    The Atlantic Salmon (Salmo salar) is a part of the Salmonidae family and can grow up to a meter in length. Atlantic Salmon have their name due to the fact they are found in the northern Atlantic Ocean. There are four life stages of the S. salar :Alevin, Fry, Parr and Smolt

    Spawning

    Atlantic Salmon begin their lifecycle by hatching in streams or rivers then gradually move out to sea once matured. When the salmon turn into adults they will return to the same location in the river or stream in which they were born in to spawn. The Atlantic Salmon is iteroparous, which means unlike other species of salmon it will travel back to the sea and return to the river in a years time to spawn again.

    Landlocked Salmon

    There is a small population of the Atlantic Salmon that only migrate to large lakes rather than the ocean. These fish are called "landlocked" Salmon.

    Fishing

    The Atlantic Salmon is very healthy to eat and happens to be very tasty therefore making it a very sought after fish. In 2023 the approximate price range for Canadian Salmon is between $5.13 and $5.37 per pound(lb). Many people used to recreationally and commecially fish for Atlantic Salmon. However this has resulted in the species becoming endangered therefore most Atlantic Salmon found in the supermarket today are from aquaculture farms.
